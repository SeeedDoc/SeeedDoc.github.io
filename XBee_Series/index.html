XBee Series

XBee and XBee-PRO ZB are the most advanced ZigBee modules available in the XBee footprint and are ideal for deployment in ZigBee networks. Available in the low-cost XBee or extended-range XBee-PRO, and utilizing the ZigBee PRO Feature Set, these embedded RF modules are interoperable with ZigBee PRO Feature Set devices from other vendors. With advanced mesh networking functionality, XBee and XBee-PRO ZB modules improve data traffic management, allow for greater node density, and provide OEMs with the ability to change firmware remotely with over-the-air updates.

XBee Chip Antenna - S1 (802.15.4)

The XBee RF Modules were engineered to meet IEEE 802.15.4 standards and support the unique needs of low-cost, low-power wireless sensor networks. The modules require minimal power and provide reliable delivery of data between devices.
Compared with Pro version, this module is lower cost and lower power, but shorter range.

  • Frequency: 2.4GHz

  • Transmit Power: 0dBm
  • Receiver Sensitivity (1% PER): -92dBm
  • Form Factor: Through-hole
  • RF Data Rate: 250kbps

XBee Chip Antenna - S1 (DigiMesh 2.4)

XBee DigiMesh 2.4 embedded RF modules utilize the peer-to-peer DigiMesh protocol in 2.4 GHz for global deployments. This innovative mesh protocol offers users added network stability through self-healing, self-discovery, and dense network operation. With support for sleeping routers, DigiMesh is ideal for power sensitive applications relying upon batteries or power harvesting technology for power.
Compared with XBee Pro DigiMesh, this module is lower cost, lower power, but shorter range.

Features

  • Regions: US, EU, AU, JP
  • Frequency: 2.4GHz

XBee Wi-Fi PCB Antenna - S6

XBee Wi-Fi embedded RF modules provide simple serial to IEEE 802.11 connectivity. By bridging the low-power/low-cost requirements of wireless device networking with the proven infrastructure of 802.11, the XBee Wi-Fi creates new wireless opportunities for energy management, process and factory automation, wireless sensor networks, intelligent asset management and more. Focused on the rigorous requirements of these wireless device networks, the XBee Wi-Fi gives developers IP-to-the-device capability.

Features

  • Regions: US, EU, AU, JP
  • Frequency: 2.4GHz
  • Positioning: Low-power serial to Wi-Fi b/g/n
  • RF Line of Sight Range: 400 ft / 120 m
  • Transmit Power: +12 to +15 dBm
  • Receiver Sensitivity (1% PER): -72 to -84 dBm
  • Form Factor: Through-hole
  • RF Data Rate: 1 to 65 Mbps
  • Hardware Series: S6
