LinkIt ONE Twilio SMS Demo - Register a Temboo account

Note: This step is part of LinkIt ONE Twilio SMS Demo.

As we will use some APIs from Temboo, we need to register for a Temboo account as well. Click to get more information about Temboo.

Click to open the page of Temboo.

Click on Sign up and fill required information, Click Try it for free.

Then, select Arduino

Click on your name, and then Applications

You will get the APPLICATION name and KEY.

Copy it to a text file, we will use them later.
